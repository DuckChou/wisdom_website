About

It's about making a connection

Wisdom-Connect is a videoconferencing facility designed to allow residents of Aged Care Residential Facilities to be able to connect with their loved ones, each other, and the outside world.

To connect aged care residents to their families, and provide a free, easy to use communication system.

To connect aged care residents to the outside world - to share the precious gift of wisdom.
